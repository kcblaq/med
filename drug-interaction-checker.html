Paracetamol Adverse Effects

Generic name: acetaminophen

Dosage forms:

capsule, capsule liquid filled, elixir, liquid, powder, solution, suppository, suspension, tablet, tablet chewable, tablet disintegrating, tablet extended release

Along with its needed effects, acetaminophen (the active ingredient contained in Paracetamol) may cause some unwanted effects. Although not all of these side effects may occur, if they do occur they may need medical attention.

Common Side Effects

  • Bloody or black, tarry stools
  • bloody or cloudy urine
  • fever with or without chills (not present before treatment and not caused by the condition being treated)
  • pain in the lower back and/or side (severe and/or sharp)
  • pinpoint red spots on the skin
  • skin rash, hives, or itching

Rare Side Effects

  • Bloody or black, tarry stools
  • bloody or cloudy urine
  • fever with or without chills (not present before treatment and not caused by the condition being treated)
  • pain in the lower back and/or side (severe and/or sharp)
  • pinpoint red spots on the skin
  • skin rash, hives, or itching
